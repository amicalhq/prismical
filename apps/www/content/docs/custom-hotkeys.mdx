Prismical allows you to configure custom keyboard shortcuts for all transcription actions, giving you complete control over your workflow.

## Default Hotkeys

By default, Prismical comes with these keyboard shortcuts:

- `fn`: Start/Stop transcription

## Customizing Hotkeys

You can customize any hotkey in the Prismical preferences:

1. Open Prismical settings
2. Go to the "Hotkeys" section
3. Click on the action you want to modify
4. Press your desired key combination
5. Click "Save" to apply changes
